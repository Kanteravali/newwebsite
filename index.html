Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pulsecraft's integrated platform.

How secure is the Pulsecraft platform?

We implement bank-level security with end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular third-party security audits. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II compliant and follows industry best practices for data protection. Cryptocurrency assets are protected with multi-signature wallets and cold storage solutions.

Can I integrate Pulsecraft with my existing systems?

Yes, Pulsecraft offers comprehensive APIs and integration tools for connecting with most popular business systems including CRM, ERP, accounting software, and custom enterprise applications. We also provide pre-built connectors for major cloud platforms, payment gateways, and blockchain networks.

What is the implementation timeline?

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity. Standard Worksuite deployments take 2-4 weeks. Cloud migrations typically require 4-8 weeks. Crypto service integrations can be completed in 3-6 weeks. We provide a detailed project plan during the consultation phase and assign a dedicated implementation team to ensure a smooth transition.

Do you offer customized solutions?

Absolutely. While our platform offers comprehensive out-of-the-box functionality, we specialize in creating tailored solutions to meet specific business requirements. Our solutions architects work closely with clients to design custom implementations, whether it's a specialized blockchain integration, custom cloud architecture, or industry-specific Worksuite configurations.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7/365 premium support with guaranteed response times. Support includes dedicated account managers, technical support engineers, and access to our knowledge base. Enterprise clients receive a dedicated customer success manager. All plans include regular platform updates, security patches, and access to new features as they are released.
